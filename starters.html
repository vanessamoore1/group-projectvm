Italian Tomato Bruschetta

Ingredients

  • 1/2 red onion, finely diced
  • 8 tomatoes, coarsly chopped
  • 8 basil leaves, finely chopped
  • 35 ml balsamic vinegar
  • 75 ml extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 large baguette

Method

  1. In a bowl, mix the onions, tomatoes, garlic and basil.
  2. Add the balsamic vinegar and olive oil.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Cover and chill for 30 mins.
  5. Slice the baguette into 12 thick slices and toast.
  6. Serve the mixture on warm bread and garnish with basil leaves.
